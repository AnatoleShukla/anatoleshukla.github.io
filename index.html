About
I'm a student at Swarthmore College with one major in economics and another in linguistics & Russian language.

I currently serve as Managing Editor of The Phoenix, Swarthmore's only independent student-run newspaper. I'm especially interested in reporting on the perspectives of students with marginalized identities, and writing theater reviews and personal essays .

Outside of school and journalism, I love birdwatching, reading about Napoleonic era history, and sewing. My favorite North American bird is probably the northern saw-whet owl, though I also love great egrets.

If you'd like to get in touch, you can also use the links to the left. You can also look around to see my writing and learn more about me!
